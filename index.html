That's me

I'm Glenn, a Front-End Developer from Vienna, Austria. I build web applications for everyone across the globe. I love working with great front-end technologies like React, focusing on quality and user experience while I learn a lot from the open-source community.

I'm an alumnus of the University of Applied Science Technikum Wien, graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Engineering with a major in Information and Communication Systems and Services. I'm a part-time freelancer, working full-time in the Styria Digital Lab team. In the past, I've built websites for clients such as ProSiebenSat.1, METRO GROUP, Volkswagen, 3i Group and more, at nexxar.

When I'm not coding, I love to spend my time with my lovely wife Charmaine, my family and friends. I enjoy traveling around the world and discover cities and cultures. I'm an Apple and Nike enthusiast who loves to play the guitar and listen to JM Music.

For more about me follow me on Twitter and GitHub. If you have any questions or just want to say hello, feel free to pop me an email.
