title: Drawing the line between 3rd party and custom code
---

Often when we write new code, we think: Come on, there must be something out there! And sometimes, someone faced the same problem already... only that it's not quite the same.

Why do we use third party libraries from npm? When do we roll our own code? How do decisions that we make impact the quality and scalability of our code?

In this talk we will deep dive into the continuous decision making we intrinsically do and explore differences and multiple scenarios.
